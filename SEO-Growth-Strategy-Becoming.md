## 5. What Competitors Are Doing That You Should Learn From

### Innertune's Playbook (Most Successful Blog in This Niche)
- Publishes **list-style posts with 80-120 affirmations** targeting specific demographics
- Every post title follows the pattern: "[Number] [Adjective] Affirmations for [Target]"
- This format ranks well because it matches how people search
- **What to steal:** The list format. **What to improve:** Add reflection prompts and depth that they lack

### I Am App's Playbook
- Creates **comparison and buyer's guide content** targeting high-intent keywords
- Positions their app as the winner in every comparison
- **What to steal:** The comparison format. **What to improve:** Be more honest and less salesy — readers notice authenticity

### MindLift's Playbook
- Uses **LLM-optimized meta tags** (targeting AI search like ChatGPT, Perplexity)
- Aggressively targets year-specific keywords ("best affirmation app 2026")
- **What to steal:** Update your content titles with year markers. Add LLM-friendly meta tags

## 6. Quick SEO Wins (Do These Now)

### Technical Fixes
1. **Add blog posts to your sitemap** — all 5 existing posts should have individual entries
2. **Optimize each blog post's meta description** with the primary keyword
3. **Add internal links** — every blog post should link to at least 2 other blog posts and to the download page
4. **Add alt text** to all images describing what they show, including keywords naturally
5. **Create a blog RSS feed** for syndication and indexing

### Content Improvements
6. **Update existing blog post titles** to include year: e.g., "Why Daily Affirmations Actually Work (2026)"
7. **Add a CTA at the bottom of every blog post** — "Try these affirmations in the Becoming app" with download link
8. **Add FAQ schema** to blog posts (you already have it on the homepage — replicate it)
9. **Add an author byline** to blog posts for E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust)

### Sitemap & Indexing
10. **Submit your sitemap to Google Search Console** if you haven't already
11. **Request indexing** for all new pages you create

## 7. Content Distribution Strategy

### Where to Share Blog Content

- **Pinterest** — Affirmation content performs extremely well on Pinterest. Create pins for each blog post with affirmation quotes as images
- **TikTok** — Short-form affirmation videos are trending massively. Read affirmations from your blog posts over calming visuals
- **Instagram (@becoming_affirmations_app)** — Share carousel posts with 5-10 affirmations from each blog post
- **Medium** — Cross-post blog articles (with canonical URLs pointing to your site) for backlinks and exposure
- **Reddit** — Share genuinely in r/affirmations, r/selfimprovement, r/journaling, r/mentalhealth (be helpful, not salesy)
- **Quora** — Answer questions like "what's the best affirmation app" and link to your comparison post

### Backlink Opportunities
- Reach out to wellness bloggers for "best affirmation app" roundup inclusion
- Offer guest posts to self-improvement blogs
- Get listed on app review sites (happierhuman.com, thegoodtrade.com, mindfulsuite.com already cover this space)
