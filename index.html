Features

Everything you need to nurture your growth.

Thoughtfully designed tools for your daily practice of self-affirmation and reflection.

Daily Affirmation

Swipe through personalized affirmation cards curated across meaningful categories. Each card is a gentle reminder of your worth.

Reflection

Pair affirmations with personal reflections. Write your thoughts, capture your feelings, and track your inner journey over time.

Create Your Own

Write affirmations that speak to your heart. Create your own categories. Make the practice deeply personal and meaningful to you.

Pick Your Categories

Choose the areas of growth that resonate with you — self love, confidence, gratitude, healing, and more. Your practice, your path.

Record Affirmation

Record yourself speaking your affirmations aloud. There's power in hearing your own voice affirm your truth.

Play & Practice

Listen back to your recordings anytime. Practice your affirmations with ambient sounds to create a calming ritual you look forward to.

Why Becoming

Built different. On purpose.

We made the app we wished existed — honest, respectful, and focused on what actually matters.

+

Not just affirmation — affirmation + reflection

Most apps stop at showing you a quote. Becoming goes further — pairing affirmations with space for reflection, so the words actually land. That's the difference between reading and becoming.

More becoming, less noise

Your moment of calm shouldn't be interrupted by a banner ad. We'll always keep the experience clean and focused — it's about your growth, not selling you things.

Affordable, always

Self-growth shouldn't come with a hefty price tag. Becoming will always stay accessible — and if we ever ask for support, it'll be on your terms and at a price that feels right.

Your data stays yours

We don't collect or sell your personal data. Your reflections, your recordings, your journey — it all stays private on your device. Period.

Let's build this together

Becoming isn't a finished product — it's a living one. We listen to your feedback, add what you ask for, and improve what matters. Your voice shapes what this app becomes. We're building it with you, not just for you.
