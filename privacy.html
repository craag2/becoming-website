Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and practices regarding privacy in connection with the use of the Becoming mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, You agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Words with capitalised initial letters have meanings defined under the following conditions. The definitions have the same meaning whether they appear in singular or plural.

Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • Application means Becoming, the software application provided by Us.
  • Company, We, Us, or Our refers to Becoming.
  • Device means any device that can access the Application, such as a smartphone or tablet.
  • You means the individual using the Application.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Data

We do not require account registration and do not collect personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number to use core App features.

2. Usage and Analytics Data

We collect limited, non-personally identifiable information for analytics purposes to improve App functionality and user experience.

This may include:

  • App usage statistics
  • Interaction events
  • Feature engagement metrics
  • General device information (such as device type and operating system version)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

This information is collected through third-party analytics services and is used solely to monitor performance and improve the App. We do not use analytics data to personally identify users.

3. Microphone and Audio Recordings

The App may request microphone access so You can record affirmation audio.

  • Microphone access is optional and only used when You choose recording features.
  • Recordings are stored locally on Your Device.
  • We do not upload or sell Your voice recordings.

4. Notifications

If You enable reminders, the App may send local notifications.

  • Notification settings and scheduling preferences are stored on Your Device.
  • Notification content is generated by the App based on Your settings.

Local Data Storage

All content created within the App (such as user-entered affirmations, reflections, settings, and optional recordings) is stored exclusively on Your Device.

  • We do not store user-generated content on Our servers.
  • We do not access or transmit Your personal in-app content.

If You delete the App, locally stored data may also be deleted depending on Your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with third-party analytics providers to help Us understand usage patterns and improve performance. These providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

At this time:

  • We do not integrate advertising networks.

However, in the future, We may integrate advertising networks or additional third-party services. If We do, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party services. We encourage You to review their privacy policies independently.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade Your information. Analytics data may be processed by third-party analytics providers solely for performance monitoring and service improvement.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in Your jurisdiction). If You believe a child has provided personal information, please contact Us so appropriate action can be taken.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect analytics data collected through third-party services. User-generated content remains stored locally on Your Device and is protected by Your device's security measures.

We recommend:

  • Using device-level authentication (passcode or biometrics)
  • Keeping Your operating system updated

International Data Transfers

Third-party analytics providers may process data on servers located outside of Your country of residence. Such transfers are governed by the privacy policies and safeguards of those providers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy within the App or on the relevant app store listing. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes become effective when posted.

Contact Us

If You have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us by email: support@becomingdailyapp.com
