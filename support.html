Common Questions

When will Becoming be available on the App Store?

We're working hard to get there. Join the waitlist and you'll be among the first to know when it launches.

Is my data private?

Yes. All content you create — affirmations, reflections, recordings — is stored locally on your device. We don't have access to it. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

No. Becoming doesn't require you to create an account or hand over any personal information to use its core features.
